Our Story

Company History

GYSAR Holdings Limited was founded in Kumasi, Ghana, by Michael Gyamfi Senior, Katara Adwoa Boaffoewaa Gyamfi, and Mcmillan Oppong Gyamfi Gyimah Junior with a singular vision: to build a diversified business empire that creates lasting value for communities.

What began as a single venture has grown into a multinational conglomerate with operations spanning nine industries. From our headquarters at the historic Anloga Onion Market in Kumasi, we have expanded our reach across Ghana and beyond, touching lives through healthcare, finance, commerce, construction, and essential services.

Under the leadership of CEO Michael Gyamfi, GYSAR continues to grow and innovate, guided by our founding principles of integrity, excellence, and community impact.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted and innovative conglomerate, setting the standard for excellence across every industry we serve — creating a future where businesses thrive, communities prosper, and opportunities are limitless.

Our Mission

To build sustainable businesses that transform lives by delivering exceptional products and services, creating employment opportunities, and contributing to the economic development of Ghana and Africa.

Timeline

Corporate Journey

2020

Foundation

GYSAR Holdings Limited was founded in Kumasi by Michael Gyamfi Senior, Katara Adwoa Boaffoewaa Gyamfi, and Mcmillan Oppong Gyamfi Gyimah Junior with a vision for diversified excellence.

2021

First Subsidiaries Launched

GYSAR Pharmacy and GYSAR Fashion opened their doors, marking the beginning of our multi-sector expansion strategy.

2022

Rapid Expansion

GYSAR Motors, GYSAR Banking Services, and GYSAR Construction joined the group, broadening our reach across key sectors.

2023

Healthcare & Home

GYSAR Hospital and GYSAR Home Appliances were established, bringing quality healthcare and modern living solutions to our customers.

2024

Infrastructure & Essentials

GYSAR Building Materials and GYSAR Nsuo Pa launched, expanding our multi-sector ecosystem.

2025

Digital Transformation

Group-wide digital transformation with e-commerce platforms, mobile banking app, and telemedicine services rolled out.

2026

ICT Solutions Launch

GYSAR ICT Solutions established, offering web apps, AI services, and mobile app development to drive digital innovation.

2026

Insurance Services Launch

GYSAR Insurance launched, offering comprehensive life, health, property, auto, and business insurance solutions across Ghana.

2026

Regional Expansion

Continued growth with plans for regional expansion across West Africa and new strategic partnerships.
