Who We Are

GYSAR Global Holdings

GYSAR Holdings Limited is a diversified multinational conglomerate headquartered in Kumasi, Ghana. With a presence across eleven key industries — from healthcare and finance to fashion and insurance — we are committed to building wealth, transforming lives, and creating opportunities across Africa and beyond.

Founded on principles of integrity, innovation, and excellence, GYSAR has grown into a trusted name in Ghanaian enterprise, serving thousands of customers daily through our network of subsidiary companies.
