GYSAR Hospital has taken a major step forward in healthcare delivery with the acquisition of advanced diagnostic equipment including a 3T MRI scanner, digital X-ray system, and fully automated laboratory analyzers. GYSAR Foundation Supports Local Education Initiative

Through its corporate social responsibility arm, GYSAR Foundation has awarded full scholarships to 25 deserving students from underserved communities in Kumasi. Each scholarship covers tuition, books, and a monthly stipend for the full academic year. The initiative is part of GYSAR's commitment to transforming lives through education.

GYSAR Nsuo Pa Expands Production Capacity

GYSAR Nsuo Pa has commissioned a new state-of-the-art bottling line that doubles the company's production capacity to 50,000 bottles per day.
